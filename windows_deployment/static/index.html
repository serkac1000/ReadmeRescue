How It Works

1
Select Video

Choose your MP4, AVI, or other video file

2
Give Voice Command

Speak naturally: "Cut from 1 minute to 2 minutes"

3
Preview Cut

Review the selected segment in the preview player

4
Save Result

Say "Save with name my_clip" to export

Voice Commands Reference

Cutting Commands
  • "Cut from 10 seconds to 30 seconds"
  • "Cut from 1 minute to 2 minutes 15 seconds"
  • "Cut from 45 seconds to 1 minute 30 seconds"
Save Commands
  • "Save with name my_highlight"
  • "Save with name best_moments"
  • "Save with name final_cut"
Tip: Speak clearly and naturally. The application supports various time formats including seconds, minutes, and combinations like "1 minute 30 seconds".
System Requirements

Supported Formats
